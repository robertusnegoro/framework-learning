## Certification Overview

### Why Get Certified?

**Benefits:**
- Validate knowledge and skills
- Career advancement
- Professional recognition
- Access to resources
- Networking opportunities
- Higher earning potential

### Certification Levels

**Foundation Level:**
- Basic ITAM knowledge
- Entry-level certification
- Prerequisites: None or minimal

**Intermediate Level:**
- Advanced ITAM knowledge
- Specialized focus
- Prerequisites: Foundation or experience

**Advanced Level:**
- Expert-level knowledge
- Comprehensive certification
- Prerequisites: Intermediate or significant experience

## IAITAM Certifications

### CAMP (Certified Asset Management Professional)

**Level:** Foundation  
**Provider:** IAITAM  
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Duration:** 2-3 days training + exam  
**Exam:** Multiple choice, 75 questions, 65% to pass

**Covers:**
- ITAM fundamentals
- Asset lifecycle
- Discovery and inventory
- Compliance basics
- Financial management

**Target Audience:**
- ITAM beginners
- IT professionals
- Asset managers
- IT support staff

---

### CHAMP (Certified Hardware Asset Management Professional)

**Level:** Intermediate  
**Provider:** IAITAM  
**Prerequisites:** CAMP or equivalent experience  
**Duration:** 2-3 days training + exam  
**Exam:** Multiple choice, scenario-based

**Covers:**
- Hardware asset management
- Hardware lifecycle
- Procurement and deployment
- Maintenance and retirement
- Hardware optimization

**Target Audience:**
- Hardware asset managers
- IT procurement
- IT operations
- ITAM professionals

---

### CSAM (Certified Software Asset Manager)

**Level:** Intermediate

### CITAM (Certified IT Asset Manager)

**Level:** Advanced  
**Provider:** IAITAM  
**Prerequisites:** CAMP + CHAMP + CSAM or equivalent experience

- Maturity assessment
- Best practices

**Target Audience:**
- Senior ITAM professionals
- ITAM managers
- ITAM consultants
- ITAM leaders

## ITIL Certifications (SACM)

### ITIL 4 Foundation

**Level:** Foundation  
**Provider:** AXELOS  
**Prerequisites:** None  
**Duration:** 2-3 days training + exam  
**Exam:** 40 questions, 60 minutes, 65% to pass

**Relevance to ITAM:**
- Service Asset and Configuration Management (SACM)
- ITAM integration
- Service management

**Target Audience:**
- IT service management professionals
- ITAM professionals
- IT operations

---

### ITIL 4 Managing Professional

**Level:** Intermediate  
**Provider:** AXELOS  
**Prerequisites:** ITIL 4 Foundation  
**Duration:** 4 modules, each 2-3 days + exam

**Relevance to ITAM:**
- Advanced SACM
- ITAM integration
- Service management practices

**Target Audience:**
- IT service management professionals
- ITAM professionals
- IT managers

## Certification Paths

### Path 1: ITAM Professional Path

**Foundation:**
1. CAMP (Certified Asset Management Professional)

**Specialization:**
2. CHAMP (Hardware) OR CSAM (Software)

**Advanced:**
3. CITAM (Certified IT Asset Manager)

**Timeline:** 1-2 years

---

### Path 2: Software Asset Management Path

**Foundation:**
1. CAMP (Certified Asset Management Professional)

**Specialization:**
2. CSAM (Certified Software Asset Manager)

**Advanced:**
3. ISO 19770 Compliance Certification

**Timeline:** 1-2 years

---

### Path 3: Integrated ITAM Path

**Foundation:**
1. CAMP (ITAM Foundation)
2. ITIL 4 Foundation (Service Management)

**Intermediate:**
3. CHAMP or CSAM (ITAM Specialization)
4. ITIL 4 Managing Professional (Service Management)

**Advanced:**
5. CITAM (Comprehensive ITAM)
6. COBIT 2019 (IT Governance)

**Timeline:** 2-3 years

## Certification Requirements

### General Requirements

**Education:**
