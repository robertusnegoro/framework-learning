## Industry Bodies

### ISACA

**Website:** [isaca.org](https://www.isaca.org)

**Description:**  
Professional association focused on IT governance, risk management, and compliance. Provides COBIT framework which includes ITAM.

**ITAM Resources:**
- COBIT framework
- ITAM guidance
- Best practices
- Training and certification

### ITIL (AXELOS)

**Website:** [axelos.com](https://www.axelos.com)

**Description:**  
ITIL framework includes Service Asset and Configuration Management which integrates with ITAM.

**ITAM Resources:**
- ITIL framework
- SACM guidance
- Best practices
- Training and certification

### FinOps Foundation

**Website:** [finops.org](https://www.finops.org)

**Description:**  
Organization promoting FinOps practices for cloud financial management, which complements cloud ITAM.

**Resources:**
- FinOps framework
- Cloud cost management
- Best practices
- Training and certification

### IAITAM Certifications
