## Overview

## Recommended Books

### ITAM Fundamentals

#### "IT Asset Management: A Pocket Guide" by IAITAM

**Description:**  
Comprehensive guide to IT Asset Management fundamentals.

**Topics:**
- ITAM basics
- Asset lifecycle
- Discovery and inventory
- Compliance
- Best practices

**Target Audience:** ITAM beginners and practitioners

---

#### "Software Asset Management: Optimizing for the Cloud and Mobile Era" by Martin Thompson

**Description:**  
Guide to Software Asset Management in modern IT environments.

**Topics:**
- Software asset management
- Cloud and mobile challenges
- License management
- Compliance
- Optimization

**Target Audience:** SAM professionals, ITAM practitioners

---

### ITAM Implementation

#### "Implementing IT Asset Management" by IAITAM

**Description:**  
Practical guide to implementing ITAM in organizations.

**Topics:**
- Implementation approach
- Planning and execution
- Tools and technology
- Best practices
- Case studies

**Target Audience:** ITAM implementers, ITAM managers

---

#### "The ITAM Handbook" by Various Authors

**Description:**  
Comprehensive handbook covering all aspects of ITAM.

**Topics:**
- ITAM framework
- Processes and procedures
- Tools and technology
- Best practices
- Case studies

**Target Audience:** ITAM professionals, ITAM managers

---

- Best practices

**Target Audience:** IT governance professionals, ITAM managers

#### "ITIL 4: Service Asset and Configuration Management" by AXELOS

ITIL guidance on Service Asset and Configuration Management.
