- Drives adoption

- Communicate value
- Regular updates

**Success Factors:**

- Demonstrated results

## Common Pitfalls

### Pitfall 1: Lack of Executive Support

**Problem:** No executive sponsorship.

**Impact:**
- Limited resources
- Low priority
- Slow adoption
- Limited success

**Solution:**
- Secure executive sponsor
- Communicate value
- Demonstrate results
- Maintain support

---

### Pitfall 2: Poor Data Quality

**Problem:** Inaccurate or incomplete data.

**Impact:**
- Poor decisions
- Compliance risks
- Limited value
- Loss of trust

**Solution:**
- Automated discovery
- Regular audits
- Data quality processes
- Continuous monitoring

---

### Pitfall 3: Tool-Centric Approach

**Problem:** Focus on tools rather than processes.

**Impact:**
- Limited value
- Poor adoption
- Wasted investment
- Process gaps

**Solution:**
- Process-first approach
- Right tools for processes
- Process optimization
- Tool support

---

### Pitfall 4: Lack of Integration

**Problem:** ITAM operates in isolation.

**Impact:**
- Data duplication
- Inconsistencies
- Limited efficiency
- Poor visibility

**Solution:**
- Integration strategy
- System integration
- Data synchronization
- Unified view

---

### Pitfall 5: Insufficient Training

**Problem:** Lack of training and skills.

**Impact:**
- Poor adoption
- Ineffective use
- Limited value
- Resistance to change

**Solution:**
- Comprehensive training
- Ongoing development
- Skills assessment
- Knowledge sharing
