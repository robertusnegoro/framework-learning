## Core FinOps Terms

### FinOps

**Financial Operations** - A cultural practice that brings financial accountability to cloud spending.

### Cloud Cost Management

The practice of managing and optimizing cloud costs.

### Cost Allocation

Assigning cloud costs to teams, projects, or business units.

### Cost Attribution

Understanding what drives cloud costs.

---

## Cost Terms

### Cloud Spend

Total amount spent on cloud services.

### Cost per Unit

Cost divided by a unit of value (e.g., cost per transaction).

### Unit Economics

Understanding the cost per unit of value delivered.

### Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)

Total cost including all factors (compute, storage, network, etc.).

### Cost Optimization

The process of reducing cloud costs while maintaining value.

---

## Allocation Terms

### Showback

Showing costs to teams without charging them.

### Chargeback

Charging teams for their cloud usage.

### Allocation Key

The method used to allocate costs (e.g., tags, accounts).

### Cost Center

Organizational unit that owns costs.

---

## Optimization Terms

### Rightsizing

Matching resources to actual needs (not over or under-provisioned).

### Reserved Instances (RI)

Commitment-based discounts for predictable workloads (AWS).

### Savings Plans

Flexible commitment-based discounts (AWS).

### Spot Instances

Using spare cloud capacity at lower prices.

### Committed Use Discounts

Discounts for committed usage (GCP).

### Reserved Capacity

Commitment-based discounts (Azure).

---

## Rate Terms

### On-Demand Pricing

Pay-as-you-go pricing with no commitment.

### Reserved Pricing

Pricing with commitment (reserved instances, savings plans).

### Spot Pricing

Pricing for spare capacity (typically lower).

### Enterprise Discounts

Volume discounts for large customers.

---

## Usage Terms

### Idle Resources

Resources that are running but not being used.

### Orphaned Resources

Resources that are no longer needed but still running.

### Over-Provisioned

Resources that are larger than needed.

### Under-Provisioned

Resources that are smaller than needed.

### Waste

Unnecessary cloud spending.

---

## Reporting Terms

### Cost Report

Report showing cloud costs.

### Usage Report

Report showing cloud usage.

### Anomaly Detection

Identifying unusual spending patterns.

### Trend Analysis

Analyzing cost trends over time.

### Forecast

Predicting future costs.

## Decision Terms

### Unit Economics

Cost per unit of value (e.g., cost per customer, cost per transaction).

### Business Value

Value delivered to the business.

### Cost-Benefit Analysis

Analyzing costs vs benefits.

### Trade-Offs

Balancing speed, cost, and quality.

## Key Differences

### Showback vs Chargeback

- **Showback:** Showing costs without charging
- **Chargeback:** Charging teams for usage

### Reserved Instances vs Savings Plans

- **Reserved Instances:** Instance-specific commitments
- **Savings Plans:** Flexible commitments

### On-Demand vs Reserved vs Spot

- **On-Demand:** No commitment, standard pricing
- **Reserved:** Commitment, discounted pricing
- **Spot:** Spare capacity, lowest pricing

### Cost Allocation vs Cost Attribution

- **Cost Allocation:** Assigning costs to teams
- **Cost Attribution:** Understanding what drives costs
