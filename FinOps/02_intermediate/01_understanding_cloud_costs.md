# Understanding Cloud Usage and Cost

**Level:** Intermediate  
**Framework:** FinOps Foundation  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

This capability focuses on understanding where cloud costs are coming from and what drives them.

---

## Key Activities

### Cost Allocation

Assigning cloud costs to teams, projects, or business units.

**Methods:**
- Tags
- Accounts
- Resource groups
- Cost centers

**Best Practices:**
- Consistent tagging strategy
- Automated allocation
- Regular validation
- Clear ownership

### Cost Attribution

Understanding what drives cloud costs.

**Key Questions:**
- What services cost the most?
- What resources cost the most?
- What drives costs?
- Why are costs increasing?

### Cost Reporting

Creating reports that show cloud costs.

**Report Types:**
- Cost by team
- Cost by service
- Cost by project
- Cost trends

**Best Practices:**
- Timely reports
- Accessible reports
- Actionable reports
- Clear visualization
