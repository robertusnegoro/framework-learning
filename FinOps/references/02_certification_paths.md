# FinOps Certification Paths

**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

This document outlines FinOps certification paths and requirements.

---

## Certification Structure

### Entry Level

**FinOps Certified Practitioner**
- Prerequisites: None
- Foundation level certification
- Covers FinOps fundamentals

### Intermediate Level

**FinOps Certified Professional**
- Prerequisites: Practitioner (check latest)
- Advanced practitioner level
- Covers advanced FinOps practices

### Advanced Level

**FinOps Certified Engineer**
- Prerequisites: Check latest requirements
- Engineering/technical focus
- Covers technical implementation

### Specialized

**FinOps for AI**
- Prerequisites: Check latest requirements
- Specialized for AI workloads
- Exam available March 2026
