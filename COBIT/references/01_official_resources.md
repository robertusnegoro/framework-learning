## Certification Information

### COBIT 2019 Foundation

- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Exam:** 75 questions, 120 minutes, 65% to pass
- **Provider:** ISACA
- **Cost:** Varies by region and membership

### COBIT 2019 Design & Implementation

- **Prerequisites:** COBIT 2019 Foundation
- **Exam:** Multiple choice
- **Provider:** ISACA
- **Cost:** Varies

### COBIT 2019 Assessor

- **Prerequisites:** COBIT 2019 Foundation
- **Exam:** Multiple choice
- **Provider:** ISACA
- **Cost:** Varies
