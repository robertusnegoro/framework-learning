**Level:** Advanced

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

This guide covers compliance and audit using COBIT 2019.

---

## Compliance Objectives

### MEA03 - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess Compliance

Objective for monitoring compliance with external requirements.

---

## Key Concepts

- **Compliance Requirements:** Regulatory and legal requirements
- **Compliance Monitoring:** Monitoring compliance
- **Audit:** Internal and external audits
- **Controls:** Control objectives and controls
- **Evidence:** Evidence for compliance

---

## Compliance Process

1. **Identify Requirements:** Identify compliance requirements
2. **Assess Compliance:** Assess current compliance
3. **Implement Controls:** Implement compliance controls
4. **Monitor Compliance:** Monitor compliance status
5. **Audit:** Conduct audits
6. **Remediate:** Address non-compliance
