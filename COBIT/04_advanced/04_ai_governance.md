- **AI Compliance:** Ensuring AI compliance
- **AI Value:** Ensuring AI delivers value

---

## COBIT Objectives for AI

Relevant COBIT objectives for AI governance:
- **EDM03:** Ensure Risk Optimization (AI risks)
- **APO02:** Manage Strategy (AI strategy)
- **APO12:** Manage Risk (AI risk management)
- **APO13:** Manage Security (AI security)
- **BAI03:** Manage Solutions (AI solutions)
