## Overview

This guide covers risk management using COBIT 2019.

---

## Risk Management Objectives

### EDM03 - Ensure Risk Optimization

Governance objective for risk optimization.

### APO12 - Manage Risk

Management objective for risk management.

---

## Key Concepts

- **Risk Appetite:** Level of risk organization accepts
- **Risk Profile:** Organization's risk profile
- **Risk Assessment:** Assessing risks
- **Risk Treatment:** Managing risks
- **Risk Monitoring:** Monitoring risks

---

## Risk Management Process

1. **Identify Risks:** Identify IT risks
2. **Assess Risks:** Assess risk impact and likelihood
3. **Treat Risks:** Implement risk treatments
4. **Monitor Risks:** Monitor risk status
5. **Review Risks:** Review and update risk assessment
