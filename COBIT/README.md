**Version:** COBIT 2019 (2018, current as of 2025)

## Overview

COBIT 2019 (Control Objectives for Information and Related Technologies) is a framework for IT governance and management. It helps organizations align IT with business objectives, manage risk, and optimize IT resources.

---

## What is COBIT 2019?

COBIT 2019 provides:
- **Governance framework** for IT
- **40 governance and management objectives** organized into 5 domains
- **Design factors** for tailoring the framework
- **Implementation guidance** for deploying COBIT
- **Focus areas** for specific topics

### Key Characteristics

- **Governance-focused:** Emphasizes governance over management
- **Flexible:** Can be tailored to organizational needs
- **Comprehensive:** Covers all aspects of IT governance
- **Integrated:** Works with other frameworks (ITIL, ISO, etc.)

---

## COBIT 2019 Structure

### Governance vs Management

**Governance:**  
Ensures stakeholder needs are evaluated, priorities are set, and performance is monitored.

**Management:**  
Plans, builds, runs, and monitors activities.

### Five Domains

1. **Evaluate, Direct and Monitor (EDM)** - Governance domain
2. **Align, Plan and Organize (APO)** - Management domain
3. **Build, Acquire and Implement (BAI)** - Management domain
4. **Deliver, Service and Support (DSS)** - Management domain
5. **Monitor, Evaluate and Assess (MEA)** - Management domain

### 40 Governance and Management Objectives

- **5 EDM objectives** (governance)
- **13 APO objectives** (management)
- **10 BAI objectives** (management)
- **6 DSS objectives** (management)
- **6 MEA objectives** (management)

## COBIT vs ITIL

| Aspect | COBIT | ITIL |
|--------|-------|------|
| **Focus** | Governance | Service Management |
| **Level** | Strategic/Governance | Operational/Management |
| **Scope** | IT governance | IT services |
| **Objectives** | 40 governance objectives | 34 practices |
| **Best For** | Governance, compliance, risk | Service delivery, operations |

**They Complement Each Other:**
- COBIT: Sets governance objectives
- ITIL: Provides practices to achieve objectives
