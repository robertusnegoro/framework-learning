## What is COBIT 2019?

**COBIT** (Control Objectives for Information and Related Technologies) is a framework for IT governance and management developed by ISACA.

### Purpose

COBIT helps organizations:
- **Govern IT effectively**
- **Align IT with business objectives**
- **Manage IT risks**
- **Optimize IT resources**
- **Ensure compliance**

### Key Characteristics

- **Governance-focused:** Emphasizes governance
- **Comprehensive:** Covers all IT governance aspects
- **Flexible:** Can be tailored to needs
- **Integrated:** Works with other frameworks

## COBIT 2019 vs COBIT 5

### Key Changes

- **Greater flexibility:** More design factors
- **Enhanced design tool:** Better tailoring capability
- **Updated objectives:** Refined 40 objectives
- **New focus areas:** Additional topics (DevOps, etc.)
- **Improved guidance:** Better implementation guidance

### What Stayed the Same

- **40 objectives:** Still 40 governance and management objectives
- **Five domains:** Same five domains
- **Core concepts:** Governance vs management remains

## Key Concepts

### Governance

**Governance** ensures that stakeholder needs are evaluated, priorities are set, and performance is monitored.

**Governance Activities:**
- Evaluate
- Direct
- Monitor

### Management

**Management** plans, builds, runs, and monitors activities.

**Management Activities:**
- Plan
- Build
- Run
- Monitor

### Governance vs Management

| Aspect | Governance | Management |
|--------|------------|------------|
| **Focus** | Direction and control | Execution |
| **Level** | Strategic | Operational |
| **Activities** | Evaluate, Direct, Monitor | Plan, Build, Run, Monitor |
| **Domain** | EDM | APO, BAI, DSS, MEA |

---

## COBIT 2019 Structure

### Five Domains

1. **EDM - Evaluate, Direct and Monitor** (Governance)
   - 5 objectives

2. **APO - Align, Plan and Organize** (Management)
   - 13 objectives

3. **BAI - Build, Acquire and Implement** (Management)
   - 10 objectives

4. **DSS - Deliver, Service and Support** (Management)
   - 6 objectives

5. **MEA - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess** (Management)
   - 6 objectives

**Total: 40 objectives**

## Governance System Components

The governance system includes:

1. **Processes:** Structured activities
2. **Organizational Structures:** Roles and responsibilities
3. **Information Flows:** Data and information
4. **Culture and Behaviors:** Values and attitudes
5. **Skills and Competencies:** Capabilities
6. **Infrastructure:** Technology and tools
