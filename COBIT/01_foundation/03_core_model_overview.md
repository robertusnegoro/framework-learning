## The 40 Objectives

COBIT 2019 defines **40 governance and management objectives** organized into **5 domains**.

---

## Domain 1: EDM - Evaluate, Direct and Monitor (Governance)

**5 Objectives:**

1. **EDM01** - Ensure Governance Framework Setting and Maintenance
2. **EDM02** - Ensure Benefits Delivery
3. **EDM03** - Ensure Risk Optimization
4. **EDM04** - Ensure Resource Optimization
5. **EDM05** - Ensure Stakeholder Engagement

**Focus:** Governance activities - evaluate, direct, monitor

---

## Domain 2: APO - Align, Plan and Organize (Management)

**13 Objectives:**

1. **APO01** - Manage I&T Management Framework
2. **APO02** - Manage Strategy
3. **APO03** - Manage Enterprise Architecture
4. **APO04** - Manage Innovation
5. **APO05** - Manage Portfolio
6. **APO06** - Manage Budget and Costs
7. **APO07** - Manage Human Resources
8. **APO08** - Manage Relationships

13. **APO13** - Manage Security

**6 Objectives:**

1. **MEA01** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess Performance and Conformance
2. **MEA02** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess the System of Internal Control
3. **MEA03** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess Compliance with External Requirements

6. **MEA06** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess Internal Control

**Focus:** Monitoring, evaluation, and assessment

---

## Objective Structure

Each objective includes:

- **Purpose Statement:** Why the objective exists
- **Goals:** What the objective achieves
- **Practices:** How to achieve the objective
- **Inputs/Outputs:** Information flows
- **RACI:** Roles and responsibilities
- **Metrics:** How to measure success

---

## Key Relationships

### EDM → Management Domains

EDM (governance) sets direction for management domains:
- EDM sets priorities → APO plans accordingly
- EDM monitors → Management domains execute

### Management Domains → EDM

Management domains provide information to EDM:
- Management executes → EDM monitors
- Management reports → EDM evaluates

### Between Management Domains

Management domains work together:
- APO plans → BAI builds → DSS runs → MEA monitors

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **40 objectives:** 5 EDM + 35 management objectives
2. **Five domains:** EDM (governance) + 4 management domains
3. **Structured:** Each objective has purpose, practices, metrics
4. **Related:** Objectives work together
5. **Comprehensive:** Covers all IT governance and management
