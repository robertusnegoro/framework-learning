**Level:** Foundation  
**Framework:** COBIT 2019  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

By the end of this document, you should understand:
- Difference between governance and management

---

## Governance

**Governance** ensures that stakeholder needs are evaluated, priorities are set, and performance is monitored.

### Governance Activities

1. **Evaluate:** Assess current state
2. **Direct:** Set direction and priorities
3. **Monitor:** Monitor performance and compliance

### Governance Domain

**EDM - Evaluate, Direct and Monitor**
- 5 governance objectives
- Strategic level
- Board and executive focus

---

## Management

**Management** plans, builds, runs, and monitors activities.

### Management Activities

1. **Plan:** Plan activities
2. **Build:** Build capabilities
3. **Run:** Execute activities
4. **Monitor:** Monitor performance

### Management Domains

**APO - Align, Plan and Organize**
- 13 objectives
- Strategic and tactical planning

**BAI - Build, Acquire and Implement**
- 10 objectives
- Building and implementing

**DSS - Deliver, Service and Support**
- 6 objectives
- Day-to-day operations

**MEA - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess**
- 6 objectives
- Monitoring and assessment

---

## Key Differences

| Aspect | Governance | Management |
|--------|------------|------------|
| **Focus** | Direction and control | Execution |
| **Level** | Strategic | Operational/Tactical |
| **Questions** | What? Why? | How? When? |
| **Domain** | EDM | APO, BAI, DSS, MEA |
| **Objectives** | 5 | 35 |

---

## How They Work Together

### Governance Sets Direction

Governance:
- Evaluates needs
- Sets priorities
- Provides direction
- Monitors performance

### Management Executes

Management:
- Plans activities
- Builds capabilities
- Runs operations
- Monitors execution

### Example

**Governance (EDM):**
- Evaluate: Assess IT strategy alignment
- Direct: Set IT priorities
- Monitor: Monitor IT performance

**Management (APO, BAI, DSS, MEA):**
- Plan: Plan IT activities
- Build: Build IT capabilities
- Run: Run IT operations
- Monitor: Monitor IT operations

---

## Key Takeaways

1. **Governance:** Evaluate, Direct, Monitor (EDM domain)
2. **Management:** Plan, Build, Run, Monitor (APO, BAI, DSS, MEA)
3. **Different but related:** Governance sets direction, management executes
4. **Both needed:** Effective IT requires both governance and management
5. **EDM is governance:** Only EDM is governance domain
