# Tailoring COBIT 2019

**Level:** Implementation  
**Framework:** COBIT 2019  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

This guide covers how to tailor COBIT 2019 to your organization's specific needs.

---

## Tailoring Approach

### Step 1: Assess Design Factors

Assess all 11 design factors:
- Enterprise strategy
- Enterprise goals
- Risk profile
- I&T-related issues
- Threat landscape
- Compliance requirements
- Role of IT
- Sourcing model
- IT implementation methods
- Technology adoption strategy
- Enterprise size

### Step 2: Determine Priorities

Based on design factors:
- Identify priority objectives
- Focus on relevant objectives
- Customize practices
- Adapt to context

### Step 3: Select Focus Areas

Consider focus areas:
- Security
- Risk
- DevOps
- Business continuity
- Small and medium enterprises
- etc.

### Step 4: Customize Framework

- Select relevant objectives
- Customize practices
- Adapt metrics
- Tailor to organization

---

## Key Principles

- **Not One-Size-Fits-All:** Tailor to your needs
- **Use Design Factors:** Guide tailoring decisions
- **Focus on Value:** Focus on value-creating objectives
- **Start Where You Are:** Build on existing capabilities
- **Iterate:** Refine over time
