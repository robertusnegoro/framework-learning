# APO - Align, Plan and Organize

**Level:** Governance Objectives  
**Framework:** COBIT 2019  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

**APO - Align, Plan and Organize** is a management domain with 13 objectives focused on strategic and tactical planning.

---

## The 13 APO Objectives

1. **APO01** - Manage I&T Management Framework
2. **APO02** - Manage Strategy
3. **APO03** - Manage Enterprise Architecture
4. **APO04** - Manage Innovation
5. **APO05** - Manage Portfolio
6. **APO06** - Manage Budget and Costs
7. **APO07** - Manage Human Resources
8. **APO08** - Manage Relationships
9. **APO09** - Manage Service Agreements
10. **APO10** - Manage Suppliers
11. **APO11** - Manage Quality
12. **APO12** - Manage Risk
13. **APO13** - Manage Security

---

## Key Focus Areas

- **Strategic Planning:** Aligning IT with business
- **Resource Planning:** Budget, human resources
- **Relationship Management:** Stakeholders, suppliers
- **Risk and Security:** Managing risks and security
- **Quality:** Ensuring quality
