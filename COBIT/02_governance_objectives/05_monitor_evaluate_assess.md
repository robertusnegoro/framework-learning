# MEA - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess

**Level:** Governance Objectives  
**Framework:** COBIT 2019  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

**MEA - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess** is a management domain with 6 objectives focused on monitoring, evaluation, and assessment.

---

## The 6 MEA Objectives

1. **MEA01** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess Performance and Conformance
2. **MEA02** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess the System of Internal Control
3. **MEA03** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess Compliance with External Requirements
4. **MEA04** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess Governance
5. **MEA05** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess Management Practices
6. **MEA06** - Monitor, Evaluate and Assess Internal Control

---

## Key Focus Areas

- **Performance Monitoring:** Monitoring performance
- **Compliance:** Ensuring compliance
- **Governance Assessment:** Assessing governance
- **Internal Control:** Monitoring controls
