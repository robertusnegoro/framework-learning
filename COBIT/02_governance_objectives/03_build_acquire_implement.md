# BAI - Build, Acquire and Implement

**Level:** Governance Objectives  
**Framework:** COBIT 2019  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

**BAI - Build, Acquire and Implement** is a management domain with 10 objectives focused on building and implementing solutions.

---

## The 10 BAI Objectives

1. **BAI01** - Manage Programs and Projects
2. **BAI02** - Manage Requirements Definition
3. **BAI03** - Manage Solutions Identification and Build
4. **BAI04** - Manage Availability and Capacity
5. **BAI05** - Manage Organizational Change
6. **BAI06** - Manage IT Changes
7. **BAI07** - Manage Change Acceptance and Transitioning
8. **BAI08** - Manage Knowledge
9. **BAI09** - Manage Assets
10. **BAI10** - Manage Configuration

---

## Key Focus Areas

- **Project Management:** Programs and projects
- **Solution Development:** Requirements, design, build
- **Change Management:** IT changes, organizational change
- **Knowledge Management:** Capturing and sharing knowledge
- **Asset Management:** Managing IT assets
