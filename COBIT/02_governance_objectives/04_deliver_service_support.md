# DSS - Deliver, Service and Support

**Level:** Governance Objectives  
**Framework:** COBIT 2019  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

**DSS - Deliver, Service and Support** is a management domain with 6 objectives focused on day-to-day service delivery and support.

---

## The 6 DSS Objectives

1. **DSS01** - Manage Operations
2. **DSS02** - Manage Service Requests and Incidents
3. **DSS03** - Manage Problems
4. **DSS04** - Manage Continuity
5. **DSS05** - Manage Security Services
6. **DSS06** - Manage Business Process Controls

---

## Key Focus Areas

- **Service Operations:** Day-to-day operations
- **Incident Management:** Handling incidents
- **Problem Management:** Finding root causes
- **Continuity:** Business continuity
- **Security:** Security services
