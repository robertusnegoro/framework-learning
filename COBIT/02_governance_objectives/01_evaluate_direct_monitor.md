# EDM - Evaluate, Direct and Monitor

**Level:** Governance Objectives  
**Framework:** COBIT 2019  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

**EDM - Evaluate, Direct and Monitor** is the governance domain with 5 objectives.

---

## The 5 EDM Objectives

### EDM01 - Ensure Governance Framework Setting and Maintenance

**Purpose:** Establish and maintain the governance framework.

**Key Activities:**
- Define governance framework
- Establish governance structures
- Maintain governance framework
- Ensure framework effectiveness

### EDM02 - Ensure Benefits Delivery

**Purpose:** Ensure IT delivers expected benefits.

**Key Activities:**
- Define expected benefits
- Monitor benefits delivery
- Ensure benefits realization
- Optimize benefits

### EDM03 - Ensure Risk Optimization

**Purpose:** Ensure IT risks are optimized.

**Key Activities:**
- Assess IT risks
- Set risk appetite
- Optimize risk-return balance
- Monitor risks

### EDM04 - Ensure Resource Optimization

**Purpose:** Ensure IT resources are optimized.

**Key Activities:**
- Assess resource needs
- Allocate resources
- Optimize resource utilization
- Monitor resources

### EDM05 - Ensure Stakeholder Engagement

**Purpose:** Ensure stakeholder needs are understood and addressed.

**Key Activities:**
- Identify stakeholders
- Understand stakeholder needs
- Engage stakeholders
- Monitor satisfaction

---

## Key Concepts

- **Governance Domain:** Only EDM is governance
- **Strategic Level:** Board and executive focus
- **Direction Setting:** Sets direction for management
- **Monitoring:** Monitors management performance
