# Technical Management Practices

**Level:** Practices  
**Framework:** ITIL 4  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

ITIL 4 defines **3 Technical Management Practices** that are technical in nature.

---

## The 3 Technical Management Practices

### 1. Deployment Management
Deploying components and services.

### 2. Infrastructure and Platform Management
Managing infrastructure and platforms.

### 3. Software Development and Management
Developing and managing software.

---

## Key Concepts

### Deployment Management
Managing the deployment of components and services to production.

### Infrastructure and Platform Management
Managing IT infrastructure and platforms.

### Software Development and Management
Managing software development and lifecycle.
