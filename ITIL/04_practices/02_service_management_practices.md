# Service Management Practices

**Level:** Practices  
**Framework:** ITIL 4  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

ITIL 4 defines **17 Service Management Practices** specific to IT service management.

---

## The 17 Service Management Practices

### 1. Availability Management
Ensuring services meet availability requirements.

### 2. Business Analysis
Analyzing business needs and requirements.

### 3. Capacity and Performance Management
Ensuring adequate capacity and performance.

### 4. Change Enablement
Managing changes safely.

### 5. Incident Management
Restoring service quickly.

### 6. IT Asset Management
Managing IT assets.

### 7. Monitoring and Event Management
Monitoring services and events.

### 8. Problem Management
Finding root causes.

### 9. Release Management
Managing releases.

### 10. Service Catalog Management
Managing the service catalog.

### 11. Service Configuration Management
Managing service configurations.

### 12. Service Continuity Management
Ensuring service continuity.

### 13. Service Design
Designing services.

### 14. Service Desk
Single point of contact.

### 15. Service Level Management
Managing service levels.

### 16. Service Request Management
Handling service requests.

### 17. Service Validation and Testing
Validating and testing services.
