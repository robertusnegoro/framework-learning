# Drive Stakeholder Value (DSV)

**Level:** Managing Professional  
**Framework:** ITIL 4  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

**Drive Stakeholder Value (DSV)** focuses on managing stakeholder relationships and driving value through services.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Understand stakeholder management
- Learn how to drive value
- Understand service relationships
- Learn about customer journey

## Service Value Chain Activities

DSV primarily supports:
- **Engage:** Stakeholder engagement
- **Deliver & Support:** Delivering value

---

## Key Concepts

### Stakeholder Engagement

Engaging with stakeholders to understand needs and ensure satisfaction.

### Value Co-Creation

Creating value together with customers and stakeholders.

### Customer Journey

The end-to-end experience of customers with services.
