# Direct, Plan & Improve (DPI)

**Level:** Managing Professional  
**Framework:** ITIL 4  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

**Direct, Plan & Improve (DPI)** focuses on directing, planning, and improving all aspects of service management.
