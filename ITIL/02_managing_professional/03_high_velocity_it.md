# High-Velocity IT (HVIT)

**Level:** Managing Professional  
**Framework:** ITIL 4  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

**High-Velocity IT (HVIT)** focuses on operating in a high-velocity environment, integrating DevOps, Agile, and Lean practices.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Understand high-velocity IT concepts
- Learn about DevOps integration
- Understand Agile and Lean practices
- Learn about digital transformation

## Service Value Chain Activities

HVIT supports all Service Value Chain activities with emphasis on:
- **Obtain/Build:** Rapid development
- **Design & Transition:** Fast transitions
- **Deliver & Support:** Continuous delivery

---

## Key Concepts

### Digital Transformation

Transforming organizations through digital technologies.

### Continuous Delivery

Delivering changes frequently and reliably.

### Automation

Automating repetitive tasks and processes.
