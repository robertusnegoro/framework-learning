# Create, Deliver & Support (CDS)

**Level:** Managing Professional  
**Framework:** ITIL 4  
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

**Create, Deliver & Support (CDS)** is one of four modules in the ITIL 4 Managing Professional track. It focuses on creating, delivering, and supporting services.

---

## Learning Objectives

- Understand how to create, deliver, and support services
- Learn about key practices for service delivery
- Understand service performance
- Learn about service quality

## Service Value Chain Activities

CDS primarily supports:
- **Design & Transition:** Designing and building services
- **Obtain/Build:** Acquiring or building components
- **Deliver & Support:** Delivering and supporting services

---

## Key Concepts

### Service Design

Designing services that meet requirements and deliver value.

### Release Management

Managing releases of services and components.

### Service Operation

Operating services to meet service levels.

### Service Support

Supporting services through incident and request management.
