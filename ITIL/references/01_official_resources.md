## Certification Information

### ITIL 4 Foundation

- **Prerequisites:** None
- **Exam:** 40 questions, 60 minutes, 65% to pass
- **Provider:** PeopleCert
- **Cost:** Varies by region and training provider

### ITIL 4 Managing Professional

- **Prerequisites:** ITIL 4 Foundation
- **Modules:** 4 modules (CDS, DSV, HVIT, DPI)
- **Provider:** PeopleCert
- **Cost:** Varies

### ITIL 4 Strategic Leader

- **Prerequisites:** ITIL 4 Foundation
- **Modules:** 2 modules (DITS, Leader)
- **Provider:** PeopleCert
- **Cost:** Varies

### ITIL 4 Master

- **Prerequisites:** Both MP and SL tracks
- **Requirements:** Experience and knowledge demonstration
- **Provider:** PeopleCert
