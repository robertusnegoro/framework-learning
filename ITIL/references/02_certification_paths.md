# ITIL 4 Certification Paths

**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

This document outlines ITIL 4 certification paths and requirements.

---

## Certification Structure

### Entry Level

**ITIL 4 Foundation**
- Prerequisites: None
- Exam: 40 questions, 60 minutes, 65% to pass
- Foundation for all other certifications

### Intermediate Level

**ITIL 4 Managing Professional (MP)**
- Prerequisites: ITIL 4 Foundation
- 4 modules required
- Focus: Operational and tactical management

**ITIL 4 Strategic Leader (SL)**
- Prerequisites: ITIL 4 Foundation
- 2 modules required
- Focus: Strategic leadership

### Expert Level

**ITIL 4 Master**
- Prerequisites: Both MP and SL tracks
- Requirements: Experience and knowledge demonstration
- Highest level certification

---

## ITIL 4 Managing Professional Track

### Required Modules

1. **Create, Deliver & Support (CDS)**
   - Service creation and delivery
   - Operational management

2. **Drive Stakeholder Value (DSV)**
   - Stakeholder management
   - Value creation

3. **High-Velocity IT (HVIT)**
   - DevOps and agile integration
   - High-velocity environments

4. **Direct, Plan & Improve (DPI)**
   - Governance and improvement
   - Planning and direction

### Exam Information

- Each module has separate exam
- Exams: Multiple choice, 90 minutes
- Passing score: Varies by module

---

## ITIL 4 Strategic Leader Track

### Required Modules

1. **Digital & IT Strategy (DITS)**
   - Digital strategy
   - IT strategy

2. **Leader: Digital & IT Strategy**
   - Leadership
   - Transformation leadership

### Exam Information

- Each module has separate exam
- Exams: Multiple choice, 90 minutes
- Passing score: Varies by module

---

## ITIL 4 Master

### Requirements

- Complete both MP and SL tracks
- Demonstrate experience
- Demonstrate knowledge
- Complete application process

### Process

1. Complete MP and SL tracks
2. Gain experience
3. Submit application
4. Complete assessment
5. Achieve Master certification

---

## Certification Renewal

### Renewal Policy

- ITIL 4 certifications have 3-year renewal period
- Renew by:
  - Retaking exam, OR
  - Taking another ITIL 4 certification, OR
  - Earning 20 CPD points per year for 3 years (PeopleCert Plus)

## Recommended Paths

### Path 1: Operational Focus

1. Foundation
2. Managing Professional (all 4 modules)
3. Optional: Strategic Leader
4. Optional: Master

### Path 2: Strategic Focus

1. Foundation
2. Strategic Leader (both modules)
3. Optional: Managing Professional
4. Optional: Master

### Path 3: Complete Path

1. Foundation
2. Managing Professional (all 4 modules)
3. Strategic Leader (both modules)
4. Master
