## Practice Exam Tips

### How to Use Practice Exams

1. **After Study:** Take after studying materials
2. **Identify Weak Areas:** Use to identify gaps
3. **Time Yourself:** Practice time management
4. **Review Answers:** Understand correct and incorrect answers
5. **Repeat:** Take multiple times

### What to Look For

- Question format
- Topic coverage
- Difficulty level
- Time requirements
- Answer explanations

## Exam Format

### Foundation

- 40 questions
- 60 minutes
- Multiple choice
- 65% to pass

### Managing Professional / Strategic Leader

- Varies by module
- 90 minutes typically
- Multiple choice
- Passing score varies

## Study Strategy

1. **Study Materials:** Complete study materials first
2. **Practice Exams:** Take practice exams
3. **Review Weak Areas:** Focus on weak areas
4. **Repeat:** Take practice exams multiple times
5. **Final Prep:** Take full mock exam before real exam
