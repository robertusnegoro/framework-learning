## Overview

This document lists online courses for learning ITSM, ITIL, and related topics. Courses are organized by provider and topic.

## Official Training Providers

### PeopleCert (ITIL)

**Website:** [peoplecert.org](https://www.peoplecert.org)  
**Description:**  
Official ITIL certification body. Provides official training and certification.

**Courses:**
- ITIL 4 Foundation
- ITIL 4 Managing Professional modules
- ITIL 4 Strategic Leader modules
- ISO/IEC 20000 certifications

**Format:**
- eLearning (self-paced)
- Virtual classroom
- Classroom training (through ATOs)

**Certification:**  
Official ITIL certifications through PeopleCert.

### ISACA (COBIT)

**Website:** [isaca.org](https://www.isaca.org)  
**Description:**  
Official COBIT provider. Offers COBIT training and certification.

**Courses:**
- COBIT 2019 Foundation
- COBIT 2019 Design & Implementation
- COBIT 2019 Assessor

**Format:**
- eLearning
- Virtual classroom
- Classroom training (through partners)

**Certification:**  
Official COBIT certifications through ISACA.

## Course Selection Guide

### For Certification

**Recommended:**
- Official PeopleCert training (ITIL)
- Official ISACA training (COBIT)
- Accredited Training Organizations

**Why:**
- Official courseware
- Experienced instructors
- Exam preparation
- Certification included

### For Learning

**Recommended:**
- Online platforms (Udemy, Coursera)
- Free resources
- Combination of sources

**Why:**
- Cost-effective
- Self-paced
- Variety of content
- Supplementary materials

---

## Course Formats

### Self-Paced eLearning

**Pros:**
- Learn at your own pace
- Review as needed
- Often more affordable

**Cons:**
- Requires self-discipline
- No instructor interaction
- May lack structure

### Virtual Classroom

**Pros:**
- Live instructor
- Interaction with instructor and peers
- Structured schedule

**Cons:**
- Fixed schedule
- Requires time commitment
- May be more expensive

### Classroom Training

**Pros:**
- Face-to-face interaction
- Networking opportunities
- Focused learning environment

**Cons:**
- Requires travel
- More expensive
- Fixed schedule

## Tips for Online Learning

1. **Set a Schedule:**  
   Allocate regular time for learning.

2. **Take Notes:**  
   Write down key concepts and examples.

3. **Practice:**  
   Use practice exams and exercises.

4. **Join Communities:**  
   Discuss with others learning ITSM.

5. **Apply Learning:**  
   Try to apply concepts in your work.

6. **Review Regularly:**  
   Review materials to reinforce learning.
