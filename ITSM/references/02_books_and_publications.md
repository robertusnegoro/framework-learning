# ITSM Books and Publications

**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

This document lists recommended books and publications for learning ITSM. Books are organized by topic and level.

---

## Foundation Level Books

### ITIL 4 Foundation

**Title:** ITIL Foundation, ITIL 4 Edition  
**Author:** AXELOS  
**Publisher:** TSO (The Stationery Office) / IT Governance Publishing  
**ISBN:** Various (check latest edition)  
**Description:**  
Official ITIL 4 Foundation guide. Essential reading for ITIL 4 Foundation certification.

**Where to Buy:**
- PeopleCert (with exam registration)
- IT Governance Publishing
- Amazon and other bookstores

### ITSM Fundamentals

**Title:** IT Service Management: A Guide for ITIL Foundation Exam Candidates  
**Author:** Various authors  
**Publisher:** Various  
**Description:**  
Study guides for ITSM and ITIL Foundation exam preparation.

**Recommended Authors:**
- Claire Agutter (ITIL Foundation Essentials)
- Various ITIL training providers

## Intermediate Level Books

### Service Management

**Title:** Service Management: Operations, Strategy, Information Technology  
**Author:** James A. Fitzsimmons, Mona J. Fitzsimmons  
**Publisher:** McGraw-Hill Education  
**ISBN:** Check latest edition  
**Description:**  
Comprehensive textbook on service management principles and practices.

### ITIL Service Lifecycle

**Title:** ITIL Service Strategy  
**Author:** AXELOS  
**Publisher:** TSO  
**Description:**  
Official ITIL Service Strategy publication (ITIL v3, concepts still relevant).

**Note:** ITIL v3 publications are still valuable for understanding service lifecycle concepts, though ITIL 4 is the current version.

## Recommended Reading Order

### For Beginners

1. ITIL 4 Foundation (official guide)
2. ITSM fundamentals book
3. Practice exam book

### For Intermediate

1. ITIL 4 Managing Professional modules
2. Service management textbooks
3. Process-specific guides

### For Advanced

1. Strategic service management books
2. Implementation guides
3. Best practices collections
4. Case studies
