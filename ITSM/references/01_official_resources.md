# ITSM Official Resources

**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

This document provides links to official resources, frameworks, and standards related to IT Service Management (ITSM).

---

## Official Frameworks

### ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library)

**Website:** [itil.com](https://www.itil.com)  
**Owner:** AXELOS (joint venture between UK Government and Capita)

**Description:**  
ITIL is the most widely adopted framework for ITSM. ITIL 4 is the current version, providing best practices for service management.

**Resources:**
- Official ITIL website: [itil.com](https://www.itil.com)
- ITIL 4 publications and guides
- ITIL certification through PeopleCert

### ISO/IEC 20000

**Website:** [iso.org/isoiec-20000](https://www.iso.org/isoiec-20000-information-technology-service-management.html)  
**Owner:** ISO/IEC

**Description:**  
International standard for IT Service Management. Provides requirements for service management systems.

**Resources:**
- ISO/IEC 20000 standard documents
- Certification bodies
- Implementation guides

### COBIT

**Website:** [isaca.org/resources/cobit](https://www.isaca.org/resources/cobit)  
**Owner:** ISACA

**Description:**  
Framework for IT governance and management. Complements ITSM by focusing on governance.

**Resources:**
- COBIT 2019 framework
- COBIT publications
- COBIT certification
